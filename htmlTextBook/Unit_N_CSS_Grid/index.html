Guava Fruit

Guava is a tropical fruit with a sweet and tangy taste. It is rich in vitamin C, fiber, and antioxidants, making it a healthy choice for snacks and desserts.

Nutritional Value

100 grams of Guava contains:

  • Calories: 68
  • Protein: 2.6g
  • Fat: 0.9g
  • Carbohydrates: 14.3g
  • Fiber: 5.4g
  • Vitamin C: 228% of the Daily Value (DV)

Health Benefits

Guava has many health benefits, including:

  • Boosts Immunity
  • Improves Digestion
  • Reduces Risk of Cancer
  • Lowers Risk of Heart Disease
  • Improves Skin Health
