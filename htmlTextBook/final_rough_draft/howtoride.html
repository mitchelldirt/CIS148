How to Ride

Getting on the train

Getting on the train is a breeze! Just be there 5 minutes prior to arrival time and walk onto the train

Paying for the train

Every doorway on the train will have payment terminals and all you need to do is tap your debit, credit, or transit card to the terminal. Alternatively, there will be payment terminals in the station you can swipe your transit card on.

Riding the train

Make sure you know which station you're at and which one you're going to. Listen to the announcements and once your stop is next get up and ready to go. As soon as the doors open you can get off as you have priority over customers getting on the train.
