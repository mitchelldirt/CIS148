About Us

GR Transit has been making people smile and saving riders money since 2005. We are Michigan's largest and most frequent public transportation system. Our goal is to provide safe, reliable, and affordable transportation to the residents and visitors of Grand Rapids.

Whether you're a daily commuter or just visiting, we have a transportation option for you. We offer a variety of transportation options such as trains, buses, and bike/scooter share. We are proud to serve the residents and visitors of Grand Rapids. Thank you for choosing GR Transit. We look forward to serving you.
