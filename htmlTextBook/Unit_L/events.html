Butterfly Season

June and July

Minnesota plays host to a variety of beautiful butterflies in high summer. As a result of our participation a region-wide effort to increase butterfly habitat by planting more native bushes and shrubs, more butterflies visit Lakeland Reeds' gardens each year. The video above shows one of our butterfly-friendly bushes covered with visitors during a typical late June/early July peak.

Accordion Rendezvous
